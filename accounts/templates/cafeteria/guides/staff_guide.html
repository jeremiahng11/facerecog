Staff User Guide

System: FaceID Portal — Cafeteria App
Role: Staff (all employees)
Last Updated: April 2026

1. Getting Started

1.1 Install the App (PWA)

  1. Open your browser and go to the app URL provided by your admin
  2. Log in with your Staff ID and Password
  3. On your phone: tap "Add to Home Screen" (Safari: Share → Add to Home Screen; Chrome: menu → Install App)
  4. The app will appear as an icon on your phone — works like a native app

1.2 Set Up Face ID

  1. Go to Profile (bottom nav)
  2. Tap Set Up Face ID
  3. Allow camera access when prompted
  4. Click Capture & Enroll
  5. The system takes 3 quick photos (~2 seconds) — look directly at the camera and blink naturally
  6. Once enrolled, you can log in using Face ID at kiosks

1.3 Set Up Kiosk PIN

  1. Go to Profile
  2. Enter a 4-6 digit PIN in the Kiosk PIN section
  3. Tap Update PIN
  4. Use this PIN for quick login at cafeteria kiosks

2. The App (PWA) Navigation

The bottom navigation bar has 5 tabs:

TabDescription
HomeWallet balance, Wallet QR (for vending machines), active order, recent orders
OrderBrowse menu and place orders
EventsBrowse and book catering events
QRShow your collection QR code(s) for active orders
ProfileAccount details, Face ID, Kiosk PIN, Order History, Logout

3. Ordering Food

  1. Tap the Order tab
  2. Browse three menus: Local Kitchen, International Kitchen, Cafe Bar
  3. Tap + to add items to your cart
  4. You can mix items from all menus in one order
  5. Tap Checkout at the bottom to place your order
  6. Credits are deducted automatically from your wallet
Mixed Orders: If your order has items from both Kitchen and Cafe Bar, you'll need to collect from both counters. Each counter will scan your QR to release their items.

4. Collecting Your Order

  1. After ordering, go to the QR tab to see your collection QR code
  2. Go to the counter (Kitchen or Cafe Bar)
  3. Show your QR code to the counter staff
  4. They scan it to confirm collection and hand you your food
Important: Collect your food within 20 minutes of ordering. Uncollected orders may be marked as "No Show" and eventually auto-cancelled at closing time, though credits will be refunded.

5. Wallet & Credits

  • Your Wallet Balance is shown on the Home tab
  • Credits are loaded monthly (e.g. S$50 on the 1st of each month)
  • Food orders are deducted from your balance
  • If an order is cancelled, credits are refunded automatically

5.1 Vending Machine (Wallet QR)

  • Your Home tab shows a Wallet QR code beside your balance
  • To buy from a cafeteria vending machine, open the Home tab and hold your phone's QR up to the machine's scanner
  • The cost is deducted from your wallet automatically
  • View vending transactions in Profile → History → Vending tab

6. Order History

  1. Go to Profile → Order History
  2. Two tabs: Orders (food orders) and Vending (vending machine purchases)
  3. See status, items, credits used, and amount for each transaction
  4. Failed vending attempts (e.g. insufficient funds) are shown in red

7. Booking Events

  1. Tap the Events tab
  2. Browse available catering packages
  3. Tap Book on a package
  4. Fill in: Event Type (team bonding, meeting, etc.), Date, Time, Venue, Number of Pax, Special Requests
  5. Submit — your request goes to the admin for approval
  6. You'll see the status (Pending / Approved / Rejected) in the Events tab

8. Using the Kiosk

Cafeteria kiosks are shared tablets near the food counters:

  1. Approach the kiosk and tap to start
  2. Log in with Face ID (look at the camera) or PIN
  3. Browse the menu (Local, International, Cafe Bar)
  4. Add items and checkout
  5. Your receipt with QR code prints automatically
  6. Take the receipt to the counter for collection
Tip: The kiosk auto-logs out after 30 seconds of inactivity to protect your account.

9. Face ID Login

  • At the login screen, tap Face ID
  • Look directly at the camera — the system scans your face
  • Login takes about 2-3 seconds
  • If it doesn't recognise you, use Staff ID + Password instead

10. Troubleshooting

IssueSolution
Face ID not workingMake sure Face ID is enrolled (Profile → Set Up Face ID). Ensure good lighting and look directly at the camera.
Insufficient creditYour monthly credit may have been used up. Check your balance on the Home tab. Contact admin for adjustments.
"Account expired" messageYour temp/intern account end date has passed. Contact admin.
Order not showing in QR tabYour order may already be collected or cancelled. Check Order History in Profile.
Vending machine rejects QRCheck your wallet balance. The vending amount may exceed your remaining credit.
